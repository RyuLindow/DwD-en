Consulting

in Denmark

Tax settlements for 2022 will begin on the 13th of March.
From the 14th of April, 2023, SKAT will begin paying tax refunds.
The time to settle the year 2022 will be to May 1. Cross-border workers until July 1, 2023.
After the 1st of July, you can get a fine for not settling with SKAT.
The deadline for paying the tax due without additional interest expires on July 1, 2023.
